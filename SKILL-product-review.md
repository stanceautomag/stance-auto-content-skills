# Stance Auto Magazine — Product Review Writer

## When To Use This Skill
Use this skill when writing product reviews for Stance Auto Magazine — covering aftermarket parts, accessories, tools, tech, or any automotive product. This skill is optimised for conversion and AI search visibility.

---

## Objective
Write as a casual, informed reviewer — conversational, personal, and friendly. Answer the questions a potential buyer would actually ask, as if talking to a friend or a class. No bullet points. Well-formed paragraphs with smooth flow throughout.

After completing a first draft, rough up the writing. Loosen sentences, drop unnecessary commas, make it sound like someone who genuinely uses this stuff rather than someone writing a press release.

These articles should also be structured to catch the eye of AI search bots and featured snippet opportunities.

## SEO Block (Always Output In This Order Before The Article)

**Slug** — Max 60 characters, lowercase, hyphens only, focus keyword included.

**Meta Description** — 160 characters max. Include the main keyword. Insightful and click-worthy.

**Keywords** — 15 total, mix of short-tail and long-tail, comma-separated. Brand-focused where relevant.

**Focus Keyword** — Single phrase. Must appear in: SEO title, meta description, slug, first 10% of body, at least one H2 or H3, and the image alt text. Maintain a natural keyword density above 0.60% throughout the article.

**Image Alt Text** — Descriptive, includes focus keyword.

---

## Article Structure

**Title Format:** [Focus Keyword] — high search-intent H1, max 60 characters. Align with how users search on Google. Use technical terms, car model identifiers, or popular phrases.

**Word Count:** Up to 3000 words.

**Headings:** H2, H3, H4 hierarchy. Include the article's main keyword in headings. Guide the reader logically through: what it is, who it's for, how it works, real-world performance, verdict.

**Body:** Narrative paragraphs only. No bullet points. Bold high-authority brand keywords contextually within the body — not just in headings.

**Tone:** Personal and friendly. Speak directly to the reader. Answer the questions they came with.

**Backlinks:** Include at least one internal backlink to a relevant article on [https://stanceauto.co.uk](https://stanceauto.co.uk) within the body text.

**External DoFollow Link:** Include one external DoFollow link to the manufacturer's official site or product page.
