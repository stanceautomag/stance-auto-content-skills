# Stance Auto Magazine — Content Creation Skills

A free, open collection of content creation skill files for automotive bloggers, magazine editors, and SEO content writers. Built and used by [Stance Auto Magazine](https://stanceauto.co.uk) — an online publication dedicated to car culture, builds, and modifications.

These skills are designed to be used with AI writing assistants (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.) to produce consistent, SEO-optimised, human-feeling content. Paste the relevant skill into your AI chat as a system prompt or instruction set, then provide your topic.

We believe in sharing knowledge freely. If these help you, use them, adapt them, improve them.

---

## The Skills

### 1. `SKILL-seo-article.md` — SEO Blog Article Writer
For general editorial articles, features, guides, and opinion pieces. Narrative-driven, immersive writing style with full SEO optimisation. 1500–2500 words.

**Best for:** Car culture features, how-to articles, event coverage, opinion pieces.

---

### 2. `SKILL-product-review.md` — Product Review Writer
For aftermarket parts, accessories, tools, and automotive tech reviews. Conversational and personal — answers the questions a buyer would actually ask. Up to 3000 words.

**Best for:** Throttle controllers, coilovers, exhausts, interior accessories, anything with a box and a price tag.

---

### 3. `SKILL-buyers-guide.md` — Buyer's Guide & Feature Article Writer
For in-depth buyer's guides, comparisons, and technical features. Follows the "Exhaust Guide formula" — immersive, richly described, built for search. 2000–2500 words.

**Best for:** "Best [product] for [car]" articles, upgrade guides, comparison features.

---

### 4. `SKILL-article-correction.md` — Article Correction & Enhancement
For correcting and enhancing submitted contributor articles while preserving the author's original voice and storytelling. Output is Google Docs-friendly for easy editing.

**Best for:** Community submissions, reader builds, guest contributors.

---

## How To Use

1. Open your AI assistant (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.)
2. Start a new conversation
3. Paste the contents of the relevant skill file as your first message or system prompt
4. Follow with your topic, product details, or submitted article
5. The AI will produce content following the skill's structure and SEO requirements

---

## SEO Block Structure

All four skills output an SEO block before the article in this order:

- **Slug** (max 60 characters)
- **Meta Description**
- **Keywords** (15, comma-separated)
- **Focus Keyword**
- **Image Alt Text**

The article body follows after this block.

---

## Writing Philosophy

Good automotive content doesn't sound like it was written by a machine trying to be helpful. It sounds like someone who actually cares about cars, who's been to the shows, who's read the forums, who gets it. These skills are built around that principle — SEO rigour without losing the human voice.

Rough up the writing. Drop a comma. Let a sentence breathe. That's the goal.
